<p><p>The packaging film market comprises various types, including PP films, BOPET films, BOPP films, CPP films, and others. PP (polypropylene) films are versatile and resistant to moisture. BOPET (bi-axially oriented polyethylene terephthalate) films are known for their high strength and clarity, making them ideal for food packaging. BOPP (biaxially oriented polypropylene) films are lightweight and offer good barrier properties. CPP (cast polypropylene) films provide excellent sealability and are suitable for flexible packaging. Other films include specialized materials for niche applications.</p></p>
