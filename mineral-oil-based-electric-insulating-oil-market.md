<p><p>The mineral oil-based electric insulating oil market is divided into two main types: high freezing point oil and low freezing point oil. High freezing point oils are designed for use in colder climates, where temperatures can drop significantly, ensuring effective insulation and preventing solidification. In contrast, low freezing point oils are more suitable for warmer environments, offering efficient performance without the risk of liquefaction. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring the reliability and safety of electrical equipment.</p></p>
