<p><strong>The Glass Fiber Tissue Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet-Laid Process</li><li>Dry-Laid Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The glass fiber tissue market consists of two primary types: wet-laid and dry-laid processes. The wet-laid process involves mixing glass fibers with water and additives to form a slurry, which is then sheeted and dried, resulting in a flexible and uniform product ideal for applications demanding high durability. Conversely, the dry-laid process involves laying down dry glass fibers onto a conveyor, which are then bonded through thermal or chemical means, offering cost-effective solutions for lightweight applications. Both types serve diverse industries, enhancing product performance and insulation.</p></p>
