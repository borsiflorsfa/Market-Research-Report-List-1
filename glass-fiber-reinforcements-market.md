<p><p>The glass fiber reinforcements market comprises several key types: </p><p>1. **Roving**: Continuous strands of glass fibers used for various reinforcement purposes.</p><p>2. **Chopped Strand**: Short fibers for composite applications, enhancing strength and durability. </p><p>3. **Chopped Strand Mat (CSM)**: A non-woven mat of chopped strands, ideal for applications needing good surface finish. </p><p>4. **Continuous Filament Mat (CFM)**: Continuous, woven fibers offering high strength and drapability. </p><p>5. **Fabrics**: Woven textiles providing flexibility and reinforcement. </p><p>6. **Other**: Includes miscellaneous forms such as preforms and specialty reinforcements.</p></p>
