<p><p>NK Cell Therapy, or Natural Killer Cell Therapy, is an innovative immunotherapy approach that harnesses the body’s immune system to target and eliminate cancer cells. These modified NK cells are then infused back into the patient to enhance the immune response against tumors, particularly in hematological malignancies.</p><p>The NK Cell Therapy Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.8% during the forecast period, driven by increasing research and development initiatives, advancements in cell manufacturing technologies, and a rise in the prevalence of cancer worldwide. <p><p>The NK Cell Therapy market encompasses two main types: NK Cell Therapies and NK Cell Directed Antibodies.
