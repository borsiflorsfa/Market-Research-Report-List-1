<p><p>The wireless printers market encompasses several key types. Wi-Fi Network printers connect directly to local networks, allowing multiple devices to print wirelessly. Cloud Printing enables printing via the internet, facilitating remote access from various devices. AirPrint, developed by Apple, allows direct printing from iOS and macOS devices over Wi-Fi without additional drivers. PictBridge enables direct printing from digital cameras to printers using USB or wireless connections, enhancing convenience for photographers. Each type caters to specific user needs and environments.</p></p>
