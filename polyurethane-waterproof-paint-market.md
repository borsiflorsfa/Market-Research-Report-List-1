<p><ul><li>Crystalline</li><li>Water Emulsion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyurethane waterproof paint market is primarily categorized into two types: crystalline and water emulsion. Crystalline polyurethane paints contain solid particles that form a waterproof barrier through a chemical reaction upon curing, providing excellent durability and water resistance. In contrast, water emulsion polyurethane paints use water as a solvent, offering easier application and lower VOC emissions. Both types are popular for various applications, including construction and industrial settings, due to their protective qualities and aesthetic appeal.</p></p>
