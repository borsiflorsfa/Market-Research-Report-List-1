<p><p>Level relay market types include Power-On Delay and Power-Off Delay relays. Power-On Delay relays activate an output after a preset delay once power is applied, ensuring systems stabilize before functioning. Conversely, Power-Off Delay relays maintain output for a set time after power is removed, allowing processes to complete safely. These relays are crucial in various industries for protecting equipment, enhancing safety, and ensuring proper operational sequences in automated systems. They contribute to efficiency and reliability in electrical applications.</p></p>
