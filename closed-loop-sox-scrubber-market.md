<p><p>A Closed Loop SOx Scrubber is an advanced pollution control technology used on ships and industrial facilities to reduce sulfur oxide (SOx) emissions from exhaust gases. This system operates by recirculating a cleaning solution, typically seawater or an alkaline liquid, to absorb SOx. It effectively minimizes marine pollution while allowing vessels to comply with stricter emission regulations, such as those set by the International Maritime Organization (IMO).</p><p>The Closed Loop SOx Scrubber Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing regulatory pressures on emissions and a rising emphasis on sustainable shipping practices. As global trade expands and environmental concerns heighten, marine operators are increasingly adopting closed loop systems to meet compliance requirements while reducing environmental impact. The innovation in scrubber technology, including greater efficiency and implementation alternatives, continues to attract investment and interest from shipping companies.</p><p>The market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.6% during the forecast period, indicating robust demand and ongoing development.
