<p><ul><li>Manual</li><li>Hydraulic</li><li>Electric</li><li>Pneumatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical examination table market comprises four main types: manual, hydraulic, electric, and pneumatic. Manual tables are adjustable by hand, offering basic functionality and affordability. Hydraulic tables use fluid pressure for height adjustments, providing stability and ease of use. Electric tables allow for precise, motorized adjustments, enhancing efficiency in clinical settings. Pneumatic tables utilize air pressure systems for adjustments, ensuring smooth transitions and comfortable patient positioning. Each type caters to different operational needs and preferences in healthcare facilities.</p></p>
