<p><strong>The Offset Printing Blankets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Compressible Blankets</li><li>Conventional Blankets</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The offset printing blankets market consists of two primary types: compressible blankets and conventional blankets. Compressible blankets are designed to provide better impression control and absorb ink, resulting in enhanced print quality and smoother surfaces. They offer elasticity for consistent pressure during printing. Conventional blankets, on the other hand, are stiffer and provide durability and reliability for high-volume printing. Each type caters to different printing needs, influencing factors like print quality, operational efficiency, and overall production costs.</p></p>
