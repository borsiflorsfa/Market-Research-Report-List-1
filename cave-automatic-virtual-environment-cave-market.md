<p><p>Cave Automatic Virtual Environment (CAVE) is a virtual reality system that projects 3D images onto the walls of a defined space. Market types include non-sealed and sealed on six sides CAVEs. Non-sealed CAVEs allow for greater flexibility, enabling users to easily interact with the environment, though they offer limited immersion. In contrast, sealed on six sides CAVEs fully enclose the user, providing a more immersive experience by eliminating outside distractions and enhancing depth perception in virtual interactions.</p></p>
