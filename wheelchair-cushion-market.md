<p><p>A wheelchair cushion is a specialized padding designed to enhance comfort, support, and pressure relief for individuals using wheelchairs. Increasing awareness around the importance of pressure relief and comfort in wheelchair users is also driving demand. Technological advancements in cushion materials and designs are enhancing product effectiveness, while a growing geriatric population is further contributing to market expansion.</p><p>The market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.30% during the forecast period. As healthcare systems prioritize patient-centered care, the wheelchair cushion market is set to evolve, focusing on improved user experiences and outcomes.</p></p>
