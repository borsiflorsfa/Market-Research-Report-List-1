<p><strong>The Super-Resolution Imaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>STED</li><li>PALM</li><li>STORM</li><li>SIM</li><li>FPALM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Super-Resolution Imaging encompasses various advanced techniques that enhance imaging resolution beyond traditional diffraction limits. STED (Stimulated Emission Depletion) utilizes focused laser beams to selectively deactivate fluorescence, allowing for high-resolution images. PALM (Photoactivated Localization Microscopy) and STORM (Stochastic Optical Reconstruction Microscopy) employ photoactivatable probes to pinpoint individual molecules with precision. SIM (Structured Illumination Microscopy) uses patterned light for enhanced resolution, while FPALM (Fluorescence Photoactivation Localization Microscopy) combines features of PALM with additional fluorescent tags for improved imaging clarity. Each method offers unique advantages for biological research and nanotechnology.</p></p>
