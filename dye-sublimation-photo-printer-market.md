<p><p>The dye sublimation photo printer market includes two primary types: thermal transfer print and direct print. Thermal transfer print involves transferring dye onto a medium via heat, resulting in high-quality images and vibrant colors, ideal for professional photography and event printing. Direct print, on the other hand, directly applies ink onto the printing surface, offering quicker setup and instant results. Both methods cater to different consumer needs, influencing their popularity in photography, event services, and retail applications.</p></p>
