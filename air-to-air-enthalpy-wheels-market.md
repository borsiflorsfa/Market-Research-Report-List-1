<p><p>The Air to Air Enthalpy Wheels market includes three main types: Condensation Rotors, Hygroscopic Rotors, and Sorption Rotors. Condensation Rotors transfer heat and moisture from exhaust air to incoming fresh air, enhancing energy efficiency. Hygroscopic Rotors utilize moisture-absorbing materials to balance humidity levels, providing comfort and energy savings. Sorption Rotors combine heat and moisture exchange through chemical processes, effectively managing environmental conditions. Each type serves to optimize energy recovery, improve indoor air quality, and reduce operational costs in HVAC systems.</p></p>
