<p><p>Remote Fiber Laser Welding Systems encompass three primary market types. Conduction Welding focuses on surface heating, ideal for thin materials, providing strong joints with minimal distortion. Laser Spot Welding is used for localized, precise bonding in applications like automotive and electronics, allowing rapid joining of parts with high repeatability. Deep Penetration Welding enables welding of thicker materials by enabling deeper energy penetration, making it suitable for heavy industrial applications. Each type offers distinct benefits catering to various manufacturing needs.</p></p>
