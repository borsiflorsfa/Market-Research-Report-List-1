<p><ul><li>Glass bead type</li><li>Micro prismatic type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sign sheeting market is primarily categorized into two types: glass bead and micro prismatic sheeting. Glass bead sheeting is traditional, utilizing embedded glass beads to reflect light, making it widely used for road signs and markings due to its cost-effectiveness. Micro prismatic sheeting, on the other hand, employs advanced technologies to enhance light reflection, offering superior visibility at wider angles and under various conditions. This type is increasingly favored in safety-critical applications, expanding its market presence.</p></p>
