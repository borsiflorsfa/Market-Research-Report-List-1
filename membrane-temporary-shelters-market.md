<p><p>The membrane temporary shelters market includes two primary types: tensioned membrane structures and inflatable membrane structures. Tensioned membrane structures utilize tension to create a stable shape, often featuring a durable outer fabric stretched over a framework, making them ideal for events and emergency relief. Inflatable membrane structures, on the other hand, rely on air pressure to maintain their form, allowing for quick deployment and lightweight designs, suitable for temporary housing and mobile operations. Both types are versatile and adaptable for various applications.</p></p>
