<p><p>The bench-top automated immunoassay analyzer market features four main types: </p><p>1. **Chemiluminescence Analysis** utilizes light emission for detection, offering high sensitivity and broad application in clinical diagnostics.</p><p>2. **Fluorescent Immunoassay** employs fluorescent labels to quantify antigens, known for its versatility and rapid results.</p><p>3. **Radioimmunoassay** uses radioisotopes for detection, providing precise measurements, though less common due to safety concerns.</p><p>4. **Enzyme Immunoassay** relies on enzyme-linked reactions for quantification, widely used for its cost-effectiveness and ease of use.</p></p>
