<p><p>The Airport Aircraft Lifting Systems market features two primary types: Air-Cushion Type and Scissors Type. Air-Cushion systems utilize air pressure to create a lifting effect, allowing for smooth elevation and maneuvering of aircraft with minimal surface contact, reducing potential damage. Scissors Type systems employ a mechanical mechanism that utilizes crisscrossed support arms, providing a stable lift, ideal for maintenance and loading tasks. Both types enhance operational efficiency and safety in airport environments.</p></p>
