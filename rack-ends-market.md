<p><p>The rack ends market consists of two primary types: threaded rack ends and blank rack ends. Threaded rack ends feature pre-cut threads, allowing for easy attachment to steering systems or linkages. These are often used in applications where precise adjustments are needed. In contrast, blank rack ends lack threading and can be customized according to specific design requirements. This flexibility makes them suitable for specialized applications. Both types are essential for the functionality and performance of steering mechanisms in various vehicles.</p></p>
