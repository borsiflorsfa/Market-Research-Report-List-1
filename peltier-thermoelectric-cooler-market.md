<p><strong>The Peltier Thermoelectric Cooler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Stage Module</li><li>Multi Stage Module</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Peltier thermoelectric cooler market is divided into two main types: single-stage and multi-stage modules. Single-stage modules utilize a single thermoelectric junction to achieve temperature differentials ideal for compact applications, such as electronics cooling. Multi-stage modules, on the other hand, consist of multiple junctions stacked to produce larger temperature differences, making them suitable for more demanding cooling needs, such as in medical devices and laser systems. This differentiation allows users to choose based on efficiency and cooling requirements.</p></p>
