<p><p>The blood collection bags market features various types designed for specific needs. Single collection bags are used for collecting and storing whole blood, while double collection bags allow for the separation of plasma and red blood cells. Triple collection bags provide additional compartments for platelets, enabling multiple components to be collected simultaneously. Quadruple collection bags further enhance efficiency by offering sections for plasma, red blood cells, platelets, and white blood cells, catering to the diverse requirements of blood banks and transfusion centers.</p></p>
