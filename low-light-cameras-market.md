<p><p>The low light cameras market is categorized based on focus technologies, including Active IR Focus, CCD AF, and Phase Difference Measurement Focus. Active IR Focus utilizes infrared light to enhance images in dim conditions, improving clarity. CCD AF relies on charge-coupled devices for high-quality imaging and accurate autofocus in low light. Phase Difference Measurement Focus offers rapid and precise focusing by comparing the phase shifts in light, enabling quick adjustments in challenging low-light environments. Each type enhances image capture under varying lighting conditions.</p></p>
