<p><p>Electric Resistance Welded (ERW) pipes are classified into two main market types: pressure tubing and standard pipes. Pressure tubing is designed for high-pressure applications, typically used in oil and gas, water distribution, and structural frameworks. These pipes ensure safety and reliability under demanding conditions. Standard pipes, on the other hand, are more versatile and cater to general piping needs, including plumbing and irrigation. Both types leverage the advantages of ERW technology, such as uniformity and cost-effectiveness in production.</p></p>
